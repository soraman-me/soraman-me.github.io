About Me

Hi, I’m Sourabh Mane, a first-year graduate student, studying Human Computer Interaction at RIT and a aspiring UX researcher. My interest lies in exploring the use of technology in health and user’s healthcare experience. I see this as a possible career as in addition to my background in computing and design, I have spent my time volunteering with a health care NGO, working with adolescent kids and health professionals.

Outside of work, I enjoy immersing myself in the experience of storytelling through videogames, watching twitch and following various esports scenes.
