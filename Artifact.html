Overview

Artifact is a card game based on Valve's DOTA2 universe. This fictional universe has more than hundred characters each with their own unique stories, goals, and rivalries. This project captures the convoluted events during the 'Battle of Roseleaf' and the characters involved, while providing a bird's eye view of the battlefield.

Year

2019

Process

Information Gathering

Although the DOTA universe has rich lore, it is fragmented and told subtly through voice lines, flavor texts, item descriptions and other mediums such as Valve events and comics. In addition to above following were the primary sources for the infomration

  1. Comic 'Prelude'
  2. Comic 'Call to Arms'
  3. Artifact card descriptions

Filtering

Information that was too specific, inconclusive and irrelevant to the plot was filtered out.

I also reached out to SirActionsSlacks & AngerMania, who are considered to be experts in dota2 lore among the community. After talking to them and making sure that I did not miss out on any significant story element, I proceeded towards the final step.

Design

As most of the events in the story take place in the distant past, I decided to go with the 'old map' asthetic. Also added some additional visual elements to improve the basic representation , added character icons to make it clearer and added just a little chaos to make it more visually engaging.
